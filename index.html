About

Hi, I'm Cesar. I'm a software engineer with a normal day job, and in my free time I make small apps.

I'm still learning a lot of the stuff that goes around building products. Marketing, design, getting people to actually find what I make. None of it comes naturally to me yet. So I keep it simple: I build something, I post about it on X, I talk to whoever shows up, and then I do it again.

I try to share the real version of how it goes, numbers included even when they're tiny. If that sounds interesting, come say hi on X.
